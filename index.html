WTF is this site?
The purpose of TBC Raid Comp is to help visualize the composition of a Burning Crusade 25 person raid, where party only Buff synergy is extremely important!

Cool Story Bro... How do i use this?
simply drag the spec or class to the raid position.
alternatively you can click on a spec or class and the first empty space available will be filled. (dragging constantly does get a little tedious)
then you can share a link or an image of the "Perfect" TBC raid comp.

Buff/Debuff information
Mouse over the specific buff or debuff for a detailed description of the ability.

Missing Buffs/Debuffs?
The scope of this site is primarily focused on offensive / damage increasing Buffs, Debuffs and some Utility.
More straightforward Buffs such as auras / totems that increase "Resistances" and "Armor" are not included.

You guys all have phones right?
Currently this site is Not optimized to run correctly on Phones.

Raid Layout?
The way i've always visualized raid groups is as follows:

Group 1: Tank     Group 2: Heal 
Group 3: Melee   Group 4: Hunter(Flex)  
Group 5: Caster

Others might do this completely differently and that's cool! -this is the arrangement i use in templates.
